Covid-19 Safety

We recognize the value of having someone with you during the consultation. However, in order to protect our patients and staff, we require that if you want to have a visitor accompany you, they be vaccinated against Covid-19

Hybrid Virtual Visits

If you have a family member or friend that you would like to include in the conversation during consultation, we can arrange for a phone call over speakerphone during the consultation.

What to Expect

During your consultation, we will review your case and the information that has been collected so far.

We may need to do a physical exam of your heart and lungs, so please wear comfortable clothing. I will provide you with my evaluation and make a plan for the next steps

Next Steps

  • Additional x-rays, such as a CT scan or PET scan
  • An endoscopic procedure
  • Placement of a feeding tube for nutrition
  • Placement of a port for chemotherapy
  • Scheduling for surgery
  • Referral for chemotherapy or radiation

Resources for You and your Family

Cancer affects not only the patient but the whole community of family and friends. We have a lot of resources: - Support groups - Counseling to help with stress reduction - Financial counselors and social workers to help with insurance and other financial issues - Supportive Oncology department to help with managing symptoms

More Resources

We have a full-time staff of dietitians

We have an Integrative Oncology program which and can assist you in choosing supplements and complementary therapies to help manage symptoms and promote wellness.

We have a senior oncology clinic which can help with the special needs of our patients over age 75
